Transforming BP Measurement

Are you measuring at the right moment?

By determining the physiological state of a patient, BP measurements become reliable.

THE PROBLEM: WHEN IS THE RIGHT MOMENT TO MEASURE?

An estimated 1.4 billion adults aged 30-79 years worldwide have hypertension, with 44% unaware of their condition (WHO).

Blood Pressure is intended to be measured when a person is in a relaxed physiological state. The World Health Organization (WHO) Guidelines mandate:

  • Relax & sit with straight back for at least 5 minutes.
  • No talking while relaxing before measurement.
  • Repeated measurements and average the readings.

All in a busy home or 10-15 minute GP consultation?

Reality Check: 77% of Doctors, 96% of Nurses do not wait and 93% of Patients do not follow the WHO measurement wait time guidelines.

OUR SOLUTION: ASSESS BEFORE YOU MEASURE

By determining the physiological state of a patient, we can drastically optimize the clinical workflow while improving:

  • Measurement Reliability
  • Patient Outcomes
  • Clinical Decisions

Our patent-pending technology (PCT) unlocks huge market potential by assessing physiological state before measurement. Using advanced algorithms and bio-signals (ECG/PPG), our platform identifies the perfect moment to take a reading, delivering a frictionless, world-class user experience.

Clinical findings suggest ECG interpeak (R-to-R) standard deviation analysis can effectively reduce the 5-minute wait to just 35 seconds.
